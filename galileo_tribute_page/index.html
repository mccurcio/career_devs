Galileo Galilei was a scientist who lived during the Age of Enlightenment. During this time observations and scientific reasoning were very important in order to understand the world. Although Galileo was not the first to build a telescope he was the first to point it toward Jupiter. By doing this Galileo showed that the universe does not orbit around the Earth exclusively. The idea is called geocentrism.

We cannot teach people anything;
We can only help them discover it within themselves.

Galileo Galilei, 1564 - 1642

A Galilean thermometer is made of a sealed glass cylinder containing a clear liquid and several glass vessels of varying mass. It can tell temperature by the change in densities.
